What makes these special

Every cake is baked fresh to order using scratch recipes, then filled, stacked, and decorated by hand. Whether you want a simple buttercream finish with florals or an elaborate fondant design, I work with you to bring your vision to life.

Custom cakes are perfect for birthdays, weddings, bridal and baby showers, graduation parties, anniversaries, and any celebration that deserves a showstopper. Every cake comes with a free design consultation so we can nail the look before I start baking.

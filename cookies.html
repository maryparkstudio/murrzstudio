What makes these special

These are not your average cookies. Each one starts with a buttery scratch sugar cookie base, cut into custom shapes and decorated with smooth royal icing in as many colors and details as you want. They dry to a beautiful matte finish that is perfect for photos.

Decorated cookies are the most customizable treat on the menu. Bring any theme: floral, vintage, seasonal, monograms, logos, and I will bring it to life. Perfect for bridal showers, baby showers, birthdays, holidays, and corporate gifting.
